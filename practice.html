Clark County Pest Control

We are a family owned business that was incorporated in Clark County in 1945. The business was started by our grandfather Frank. He served in the Army for three years in the South Pacific. He participated in the attack on several of the islands in that region.

Right after the end of the war he was discharged and started the business with a 1933 Ford pickup and a backpack sprayer. He traveled around the county knocking on doors and looking for business. He also had booths at the Clark County Fair, the Skamania County Fair and the Pacific International Livestock Exposition.

In 1965 my sister Jan and I joined the business. Currently we have three trucks and a crew of five. My son Chad and Jan's daughter Brenda are also involved with the company at this time.
